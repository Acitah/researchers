About Us

Dr. Eleanor M. Bennett

Chief Executive Officer – Researcher Portal Group

Dr. Eleanor M. Bennett is the visionary founder and CEO of the Researcher Portal Group. With over 15 years of experience in scientific research and academic leadership, she has been instrumental in shaping the platform into a global hub for collaboration and knowledge sharing.

She holds a Ph.D. in Biomedical Informatics from the University of Oxford and has authored numerous publications in the fields of healthcare technology and data-driven research. Her mission is to bridge the gap between researchers across disciplines and promote open-access scientific advancement.

“The future of science lies not just in discovery, but in how we share, connect, and empower one another to think bigger.”
