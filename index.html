About Us

Our Mission

To conduct high-impact research in machine learning and artificial intelligence, and to share our findings openly with the global scientific community through publications, datasets, and educational tools.

To provide access to datasets, trained models, or model repositories for the ML community to explore and build upon.

To encourage community involvement, invite contributors to open-source ML tools or join collaborative research.
